Face Photo Guide & Upload

Follow the guide below to take and upload your face photos.

1. Front

Look straight ahead,
show your full face

2. Side

Face completely sideways,
show your side profile line

Camera Settings

  • Use default camera mode (no wide angle)
  • Rear camera recommended
  • Camera height: between eyes and mouth
  • Distance: slightly more than arm's length (60cm)

Lighting & Background

  • Shoot in bright area (natural light best)
  • Light source facing your face
  • Avoid backlighting
  • Simple background (white, beige recommended)

Clothing & Accessories

  • White or beige solid-colored top recommended
  • Round neckline showing shoulders
  • No patterns or bold colors
  • Remove sunglasses, hats, earrings

Accuracy Tip

Photo quality directly affects analysis accuracy. Lighting (natural frontal light) and background (simple, bright) are the most important factors.

Face Video Guide & Upload

Follow the guide below to record and upload your face video.

Video Guide

  • Lighting: shoot in bright natural light
  • Angle: slowly turn from front to sides
  • Speed: not too fast
  • Duration: about 5-10 seconds

Recording Tips

  • Hold phone vertically
  • Keep 1-1.5m distance from camera
  • Choose a simple background
  • File size under 50MB

Body Photo Guide & Upload

Follow the guide below to take and upload your body photos.

1. Front

Look straight ahead,
show your full body

2. Side

Face completely sideways,
show your silhouette

Camera Settings

  • Use default camera mode
  • Camera at waist-chest height
  • Distance: full body visible (2-3m)
  • Keep camera vertical

Outfit

  • Wear fitted clothing (not too loose)
  • Solid-colored clothes recommended
  • Clothes that show body lines
  • Minimize accessories

Posture

  • Stand upright
  • Arms naturally at your sides
  • Include full body from head to toe
  • Center yourself in the frame

Accuracy Tip

Photo quality directly affects analysis accuracy. Lighting (natural frontal light) and background (simple, bright) are the most important factors.
